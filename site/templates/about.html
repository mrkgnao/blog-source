About me

I am an avid user and advocate of Arch Linux, and enjoy using text-based interfaces and Unix command-line and stream processing tools. I am a productive user of Spacemacs, a project that brings the efficiency of the Vim text-editing language to Emacs.

Among other things, I'm interested in and enjoy thinking about expressive type systems, classical music, jazz(-fusion), post-rock, "literature" (especially magical realism), "hard" science fiction, and psychology, and also sometimes indulge in aimless cogitation about AI, consciousness, and other such things.

I currently live in Kolkata, India.
